# Privacy Policy for ZenFocus New Tab

**Last Updated:** November 21, 2025

## Introduction
ZenFocus New Tab ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our browser extension handles your information.

## Data Collection and Usage
**We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.**

*   **Settings & Preferences:** Your customization settings (such as background color, timer preferences, and clock format) are stored locally on your device using your browser's storage capabilities (`chrome.storage` or `localStorage`). This data never leaves your browser and is not sent to any external servers.
*   **Search Suggestions:** When you use the search bar, your search queries are sent directly to Google's suggestion service (`suggestqueries.google.com`) to provide autocomplete suggestions. We do not intercept, store, or analyze these queries.

## Third-Party Services
*   **Google Search:** The search functionality redirects you to Google Search. Your interactions with Google are governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

## Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.

## Contact Us
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at the support email provided in the extension store listing.
